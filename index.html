partnership that brings together the simplicity and
innovation of Uber with Thames Clippers’ long-standing
tradition of safe, reliable travel on the River Thames.*


Check out the Uber app to browse the many available routes

Guaranteed seat when on board


There are never more passengers on board any
boat than the number of available seats. So
every passenger has plenty of space to relax or
catch up on work.



New commute, same simple app


Just purchase your ticket in the Uber app, turn
up at the pier, and you’re ready to sail.



Extensive coverage


With 24 piers across London and an extensive
service in both directions, you may be surprised
at how much of your commute could be
covered by boat.

How Uber Boat by Thames Clippers works

1.Book


Use the Uber app to book and pay for your trip,
with 24 piers to choose from.

2. Board


Make your way to your chosen pier and wait for
the scheduled service to arrive.

3. Sail


Sit back and relax with a guaranteed seat on
board and make your way to work.
